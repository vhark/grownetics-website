§ 001 · Why It Matters

Five problems costing you every harvest.

These aren't problems you can solve by working harder. They're structural — built into how most facilities measure, control, and learn. Grownetics is built specifically to eliminate all five.

01

The Measurement Gap

You can't control what you can't measure — and most facilities barely measure. One or two sensors per room. Air temperature at a single point. No leaf temperature, no canopy PAR, no substrate WC. CropVision gives you 10x the resolution you have today.

→ CropVision
02

The Control Precision Gap

CEA runs on thermostats. Plants need predictive, multi-variable control — one that reads rate of change, models where the environment is going, and positions equipment before it gets there. Reactive systems chase. Predictive systems prevent.

→ Unity / HiFEC
03

The Knowledge Bottleneck

Your best grower has 20 years of intuition that can't be written down, transferred, or scaled. When they leave, it leaves with them. A cultivation OS captures what experts know and makes it accessible to every operator on every shift.

→ Grownetics OS
04

The Cycle Time Problem

Every day a plant sits in your facility is cost without revenue. Eliminating the veg phase alone delivers 30% less electricity, 37% less labor, and 50% more harvests per year — but only if your measurement and control are precise enough to support it.

→ CropVision + Unity
05

The Data Continuity Gap

Every harvest starts from scratch because nobody recorded what actually happened in the last one. A batch biography — every sensor reading, recipe change, and observation correlated to final yield — turns cultivation from intuition into a compounding learning system.

→ Grownetics OS
