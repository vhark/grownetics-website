§ 001 · Five Problems Costing You Every Harvest

Your growers aren't the problem. Your tools are.

These aren't problems you solve by working harder. They're structural — built into how most facilities measure, control, and learn. Grownetics is built to eliminate all five.

The grower isn't broken. The system is. And fixing it doesn't start with another tool. It starts with a complete one.

01
The Measurement Gap

You have sensors. But do you have a 3D picture of every zone? Temperature at canopy isn't temperature at substrate. The gap between your readings and reality costs you yield.

That's 80% of your environmental variability — and the yield loss that comes with it.

→ CropVision
02
The Control Precision Gap

If your climate control is reacting to problems, it's already too late. Thermostats chase excursions. Predictive controllers prevent them.

Reactive controls waste 20–30% more energy than predictive. Every month.

→ Unity / HiFEC
03
The Knowledge Bottleneck

Your best grower has intuition that took years to build and can't be transferred. When they leave, the knowledge goes with them.

One resignation costs you years of institutional knowledge and months of recovery.

→ Grownetics OS
04
The Cycle Time Problem

Every day a plant sits in your facility is cost without revenue. Eliminating the veg phase delivers 30% less electricity, 37% less labor, 50% more harvests per year — but only if measurement and control are precise enough.

Every unnecessary day in veg is revenue you'll never recover.

→ CropVision + Unity
05
The Data Continuity Gap

Every harvest starts from scratch because nobody recorded what actually happened in the last one. A batch biography turns cultivation into a compounding learning system.

Your 50th harvest should be dramatically better than your first. Is it?

→ Grownetics OS
